Why Ostra?

**Ostra Linux: Gaming Powerhouse Meets Everyday Convenience.** Built upon the stable foundation of Ubuntu, Ostra is a Linux distribution meticulously optimized for both gaming and daily tasks. Gain complete control over your system with an intuitive graphical interface and a powerful terminal.


Why is Ostra ideal for gaming? **OPTIMIZATION.** Our custom Linux build delivers a noticeable FPS boost, unleashing your hardware's full potential. Prepare for a new level of gaming experience!
